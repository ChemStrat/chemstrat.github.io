    Methods: CAS

    Preparation

    • 10 mg Sample dissolved in 5ml 4% HNO3, shake in ultrasonic bath, 90 mins, leave it to react overnight, add x ml MQ and/or 2% HNO3 (so that [Ca] < 50 ppm), measure Mg, Ca by ICP-OES for calculating the quantity of “excess Leacher4”

    Reagents

    • Leacher1: 12% NaOCl

      • Recipe: ? grade ?% NaOCl (Fisher Catalog# ?) : MQ = ? : ?
      • Shelf life:

    • Leacher2: 1% H2O2

    • Leacher3: 10% NaCl
    • Leacher4: 6M HCl
    • Precipitant1: 250 g/L (nearly saturated) BaCl2
      • Notes: use Ba(NO3)2 instead? because Cl- (ionic strength) increases the solubility of BaSO4

    Predefined Processes

    • CheckS, either way:
      • centrifuge → Aliquot ? ml → add ? ml Leacher4 & ? ml Precipitant1
      • centrifuge → Aliquot ? ml → measuring S with ICP-OES
    • PrecipitateBaSO4:
      1. heating 85°C, 1h
      2. swirl gently
      3. cooling in fridge, 10 °C, 2h
      4. room T, >=3 days
      5. if there is no precipitate, add 2 mg of isotopic grade S-free quartz powder

    %% usually x=5–10, net result: 1–5 mg BaSO4 for "Analyse2"
    Sample(["x g Sample (140–700?? ng CAS)"])

    %% --------------- Leach1
    %% submerge sample powder by Leacher1
    Sample --> Leach1["Step1: 2–4x mL Leacher1, room T, Shake 24h"]:::Leach --> Centrifuge1 --> Result1{Result1}

    %% |"After 3×Leach1"|
    Result1 -->|Leachate1: sulphide/organic matters| CheckS1[[CheckS]]
    %% how to discard??
    CheckS1 -->|Leachate1| Discard1([Discard1])
    CheckS1 --> CheckS1Result{Yes/No}

    Result1 -->|Residual1| Rinse1["Rinse with MQ & Centrifuge"] -->|3| Rinse1 -..-> CheckS1Result

    CheckS1Result -->|"Yes (Residual1, Repeat Leach1 >=3)"| Leach1

    %% --------------- Leach2
    CheckS1Result -->|"No (Residual1)"| Leach2["Step2: 2–4x mL Leacher2, room T, Shake 2–24??h"]:::Leach --> Centrifuge2 --> Result2{Result2}
    Result2 ---|"Leachate2: sulphide/organic matters??"| Discard2([Discard2])
    Result2 -->|Residual2| Rinse2["Rinse with MQ & Centrifuge"] -->|3| Rinse2 --> Dry1["Dry 80°C, ~72?h"]

    %% --------------- Leach3
    %% submerge sample powder by Leacher3
    Dry1 --> Leach3["Step3: 2–4x mL Leacher3, room T, Shake 24h"]:::Leach --> Centrifuge3 --> Result3{Result3}

    Result3 -->|Leachate3: soluble S-bearing constituents| CheckS2[[CheckS]]
    CheckS2 -->|Leachate3| Discard3([Discard3])
    CheckS2 --> CheckS2Result{Yes/No}

    Result3 -->|Residual3| Rinse3["Rinse with MQ & Centrifuge"] -->|3| Rinse3 -..-> CheckS2Result

    CheckS2Result -->|"Yes (Residual3)"| Leach3

    %% --------------- Leach4
    %% <30 mins to prevent oxidation of sulphides
    CheckS2Result -->|"No (Residual3)"| Leach4["Step4: excess Leacher4, room T, <30 mins"]:::Leach --> Filter["Centrifuge & Filter, 0.2 µm PP membrane syringe filter"] --> Result4{Result4}
    Result4 -->|Residual4| Discard4([Discard4])
    Result4 --> Leachate4[/Leachate4/]
    Leachate4 --> Aliquot1["Aliquot1 1 ml, add 4 ml 2% HNO3"] --> Analyse1[["Analyse1 S @ICP-OES"]]:::Analyse

    %% --------------- Precipitate1
    Leachate4 --> Precipitate1["Step5: x/5 ml Precipitant1"]:::Precipitate --> PrecipitateBaSO4[[PrecipitateBaSO4]] --> Centrifuge4 --> Result5{Result5}
    Result5 ---|Supernatant1| Discard5([Discard5])
    Result5 --> Precipitates1[/Precipitates1/]
    Precipitates1 --> Leach5["~5?? ml Leacher4"] --> |Leachate5: BaCO3| Discard6([Discard6])
    Leach5 -->|Residual5| Rinse4["Rinse with MQ & Centrifuge"] -->|3| Rinse4 --> |Supernatant2: salts, including BaCl2| Discard7([Discard7])
    Rinse4 -->|Residual6: CAS in BaSO4| Dry2["Dry 60°C, ~48h"] --> Weigh1 --> Analyse2[["Analyse2 δ34S @EA-IRMS"]]:::Analyse
